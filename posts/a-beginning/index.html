A beginning

August 29, 2021

Essence

These few lines represent the beginning of an expression conveyed through the written word. Or perhaps the typed word.
I have been actively using the internet for more than 20 years now and first envisioned putting thoughts of mine in a public space at least 15 years ago.
That intent never transitioned from potential into actual, until now.
Hopefully now my thoughts and humble investigations into reality will prove of use to others and, let’s hope, to myself as well.
Hence reactiveness might transform into proactiveness, for once.

Delving into systems and extracting meaningful information out of them is an integral part of what, and who, I am.
My interest lies, often enough, in the abandoned, the obsolete, as well as in the misunderstood, the cryptic and the laid-aside.
And the exploration of its significance.
Part of it being the insights it provides in the quintessence of the whole.
By profession, and choice, I deal in the intricacies of digital systems and information and as such much of the contents here will revolve around that large segment of our contemporary reality.
